Acceptable Use

Use Browso responsibly. Do not use browser automation to harm people, compromise systems, or evade the rules of services you access.

Prohibited activity

You may not use Browso to:

  • Break the law, facilitate crime, or violate another person’s rights.
  • Access accounts, devices, networks, or data without authorization.
  • Deploy malware, phishing, credential theft, spam, or denial-of-service activity.
  • Exploit, harass, stalk, threaten, or deceive people.
  • Generate or distribute child sexual abuse material or other illegal sexual content.
  • Bypass access controls, rate limits, robots directives, or platform safeguards.
  • Manipulate markets, commit fraud, or automate deceptive transactions.
  • Interfere with Browso, probe its security without permission, or resell unauthorized access.

High-impact decisions

Do not rely on Browso as the sole decision-maker in employment, housing, credit, insurance, education, healthcare, legal services, or other areas where an error could materially affect a person’s rights or safety.

Human review

Review proposed actions before approval, especially purchases, messages, account changes, data deletion, bookings, or submissions. You remain responsible for the final action.

Enforcement

We may investigate suspected violations and restrict or terminate access when reasonably necessary. Serious threats may be reported to relevant authorities where required or permitted by law.

Reporting

Report suspected abuse through the Browso GitHub issue tracker. Do not include passwords, private keys, or sensitive personal data in a public issue.
