Privacy Policy

This policy explains the information Browso may process through this website and the Browso browser when it becomes available.

Website information

The current website does not provide user accounts or downloads. It stores your light or dark theme preference in your browser’s local storage. Hosting and network providers may process standard request data such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamps, and security events.

Browser information

When Browso launches, it may process prompts, page content, browser interactions, settings, diagnostics, and information required to perform requested actions. This policy will be updated before collection practices materially change.

How information is used

Information may be used to operate and secure Browso, complete requested actions, remember preferences, diagnose failures, prevent abuse, improve performance, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

Information may be shared with service providers that support hosting, security, diagnostics, and product operation; with third-party websites you instruct Browso to access; or when required to comply with law or protect rights and safety. We do not sell personal information.

Retention and security

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, subject to legal and operational requirements. We use reasonable safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can clear the website theme preference through your browser storage controls. Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal information.

Children

Browso is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information through this website.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy as Browso develops. Questions may be submitted through the Browso GitHub issue tracker. Do not post sensitive personal information publicly.
