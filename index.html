Frequently asked questions

About Browso.

What is Browso?

Browso is an AI browser with a built-in agent. Ask it to search websites, compare information, and complete browser tasks while you remain in control.

Which operating systems will Browso support?

Browso is being developed for macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Is Browso available to download?

Not yet. Browso is currently in development, and desktop downloads are coming soon.
