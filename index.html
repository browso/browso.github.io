Frequently asked questions

About Browso.

What is Browso?

Browso is an AI browser with a built-in agent. Ask it to search websites, compare information, and complete browser tasks while you remain in control.

Which operating systems will Browso support?

The Browso beta is available for macOS and 64-bit Windows. Linux support is coming soon.

Is Browso available to download?

Yes. Browso 1.0.0 beta is available for Apple Silicon Macs, Intel Macs, and 64-bit Windows.
